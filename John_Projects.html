MIT App Inventor Project
September 23, 2019
As a Freshman in Dublin High, my elective class is Intro to Computer Science Principles and one of the projects we made was using App Inventor. My partner Jason and I improved a simple tutorial of a Space Invaders game. We enhnaced the goals, the features, fixed the small bugs, and created the small tutorial to an actual fun and amazing game.
Instructions to SPACE INVADERS
Get the Rocket to shoot bullets at the saucer to get points. Make sure you hit the start button first before playing. Avoid the asteroid because if the bullet hits the asteroid it will subtract points from your score. Drag the rocket to move it left/right, and tap on it/let go to fire. Try to get a high score in 30 seconds!

Scratch Project
September 9, 2019
Another project we created in this class was one made on Scratch. We were given around a month and a partner to complete
a fun and creative game. My partner, Kaif and I created the game "Unnatural Area". The goal of the game was to win the duel
against your opponent and each level would get harder.
Instructions to UNNATURAL ARENA
This is a 2 Player Game that consists of three rounds. One player can choose to be the Hero (Hunter or Knight) and the other will be the Monster (Cyclops or Devil). Each class has their own benefits and downsides, so figure out which one adheres best to your play style.
Controls:
Hero: Arrow Keys
Monster: WASD Keys
